Pop Culture Quiz

Popular culture, or pop culture, in its entirety, is of attitude, ideas, images, perspectives, and other phenomena within the mainstream of a given culture. Heavily influenced by mass media, this collection of ideas permeates the everyday lives of society. The most common pop culture categories are entertainment (such as movies, music, television, and video games), sports, news, politics, fashion/clothes, technology, literature and art. So if you think you know it all, then maybe it’s time to test your knowledge in this field. Come over in a team of 2 and win it all! A quiz on culture, literature, arts, movies, T.V shows. Parliamentary Debate

“Without debate, without criticism, no administration and no country can succeed and no republic can survive.” Parliamentary Debate is modelled based on the British Parliamentary System. It’s more oratorical, witty, and accessible to general audiences, featuring lesser jargons and fewer theoretical arguments. As members of the House, each team will stand for either Proposition or Opposition. In the same way as the Parliament, the Proposition supports the motion while the Opposition opposes it. Here we call upon the masters and the amateurs in the field of parliamentary debate to come over in a team of 3-4 members and debate until you resolve!
