Shutter Blink

If it can be written or thought, it can be filmed. Film making is the art capturing little moments and weaving a story from it. It truly is a miracle of collaboration and art! So what are you waiting for? Got your teammates and a big short-film plan, here is the contest to test your skills. Shoot a short film in 48 hours and let your movie mingle with the audience’s brains. Topics would be provided to the contestants 48 hours before the starting of the festival and they have to make 3-6 minutes short- film in the provided duration. Test your skills, ace the chance. Put your Cine-scope in position, and get ready to shoot! By making the most of limited resources and acting with your friends, this event is definitely not to be missed for those who are willing to narrate the essence of life in a film.
